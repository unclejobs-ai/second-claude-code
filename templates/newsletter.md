# Newsletter Template

6-stage Yes-But-Therefore narrative structure.

---

## 1. Title

> [Curiosity-driven title that makes a specific promise or poses a bold question]

Rules:
- Must create a curiosity gap or make a bold claim
- Include a specific detail (number, name, timeframe)
- Under 15 words

## 2. Intro

> [2-3 sentences that hook the reader and establish why this topic matters right now]

Rules:
- Open with a surprising fact, question, or vivid scene
- Connect to something the reader cares about today
- End with an implicit promise of what they will learn

## 3. Yes (Agreement)

> [Establish common ground -- the thing everyone already believes or accepts]

Rules:
- Acknowledge the prevailing wisdom or shared experience
- Use phrases like "We all know...", "The standard advice is...", "Everyone agrees that..."
- Build genuine agreement before introducing tension
- 2-4 paragraphs with supporting evidence

## 4. But (Tension)

> [Introduce the twist -- the surprising reality, new data, or counter-evidence]

Rules:
- This is the core of the newsletter. Spend the most space here.
- Present specific data points, case studies, or expert perspectives that challenge the Yes
- Do not strawman the Yes section -- the tension must be real
- 3-5 paragraphs with at least 2 data points from research

## 5. Therefore (Resolution)

> [The insight or actionable takeaway that emerges from the Yes-But tension]

Rules:
- Synthesize, do not just summarize
- The Therefore must be a genuine new understanding, not a compromise
- Include at least one concrete, actionable step the reader can take
- 2-3 paragraphs

## 6. Conclusion

> [Closing takeaway + clear call to action]

Rules:
- One sentence summary of the core insight
- Specific CTA (reply, share, try something, read more)
- Optional: preview of next issue's topic

---

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Title creates curiosity gap with a specific detail
- [ ] Intro hooks within the first sentence
- [ ] Yes section is genuine, not a strawman
- [ ] But section has at least 2 data points from research
- [ ] Therefore offers a genuinely new insight
- [ ] CTA is specific and actionable
- [ ] Total word count is at least 2000 words
- [ ] Voice matches the selected tone guide
