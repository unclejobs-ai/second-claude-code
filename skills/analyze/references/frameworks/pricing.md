# Pricing Framework

## When to Use
- When reviewing pricing strategy and packaging structure
- When comparing value, segment willingness, and competitive anchors together

## When NOT to Use
- When the value proposition is still undefined
- When only cost accounting is required

## Required Sections
1. Value metric
2. Segment willingness to pay
3. Competitive anchors
4. Packaging / tiers
5. Pricing risks

## Evidence Expectations
- Tie pricing to customer value and segment evidence
- Present competitive anchors alongside differentiation points

## Recommended Actions
- Recommend 3 pricing or packaging experiments with priorities
