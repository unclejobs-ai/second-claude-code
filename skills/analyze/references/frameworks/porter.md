# Porter's Five Forces Framework

## When to Use
- When evaluating industry structure and competitive pressure
- When comparing the external forces that shape profitability

## When NOT to Use
- When analyzing a single feature UX problem
- When focusing only on internal organizational capability

## Required Sections
1. Rivalry among competitors
2. Threat of new entrants
3. Threat of substitutes
4. Bargaining power of buyers
5. Bargaining power of suppliers

## Evidence Expectations
- Include evidence and profitability impact for each force
- Do not force equal weight across all forces

## Recommended Actions
- Recommend 3 defense or attack priorities
