# Value Proposition Framework

## When to Use
- When defining which customer problem the product solves best and why
- When connecting messaging, positioning, and product focus

## When NOT to Use
- When customer segments are still too broad or vague
- When the need is only an internal execution plan

## Required Sections
1. Customer jobs
2. Pains
3. Gains
4. Product pain relievers
5. Product gain creators
6. Value proposition statement

## Evidence Expectations
- Map customer pains to product relief clearly
- Use concrete benefits instead of abstract adjectives

## Recommended Actions
- Recommend 3 messaging or product-focus improvements
