# Battlecard Framework

## When to Use
- When aligning competitive messaging against a specific rival
- When sales, marketing, and PM need a shared view of the competition

## When NOT to Use
- When the market itself still needs first-pass diagnosis
- When there is no internal product strategy to anchor the comparison

## Required Sections
1. Competitor snapshot
2. Win themes
3. Loss risks
4. Objection handling
5. Proof points

## Evidence Expectations
- Explain customer value differences, not just feature checklists
- Ground objection handling in real competitive claims

## Recommended Outputs
- Recommend 3 field-ready messages plus phrases to avoid
