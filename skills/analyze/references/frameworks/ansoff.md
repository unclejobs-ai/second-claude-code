# Ansoff Matrix Framework

## When to Use
- When comparing growth paths across the product/market matrix
- When reviewing both expansion and new-business options together

## When NOT to Use
- When market diagnosis is still more urgent than prioritizing growth bets
- When the issue is operational rather than strategic growth direction

## Required Sections
1. Market Penetration
2. Market Development
3. Product Development
4. Diversification
5. Risk comparison

## Evidence Expectations
- Include at least 2 market signals per quadrant
- State both current capability and risk

## Recommended Actions
- Recommend the 3 most realistic growth paths with priorities
