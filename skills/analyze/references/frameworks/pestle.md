# PESTLE Framework

## When to Use
- When assessing macro changes that affect a business
- When regulation, economics, or technology shifts matter to the industry

## When NOT to Use
- When solving an internal operations problem
- When only competitor comparison is needed

## Required Sections
1. Political
2. Economic
3. Social
4. Technological
5. Legal
6. Environmental

## Evidence Expectations
- Include real external signals and how they affect the business
- Keep low-signal sections brief and go deeper on major variables

## Recommended Actions
- Recommend responses to the 3 most important external shifts
