# Voice Guide: Expert

## Tone
- Authoritative without sounding arrogant
- Uses measured, evidence-led language
- Grounds claims in data and sourced reasoning

## Avoid
- Casual filler language
- Emotional hype
- Unsourced statistics

## Require
- Source or data support for every major claim
- Explicit limitations and counterarguments
- Domain vocabulary when appropriate
- Clear logic: premise → evidence → conclusion

## Sentence Length
- Medium-length sentences are fine
- Complex sentences are acceptable if they stay readable
