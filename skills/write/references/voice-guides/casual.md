# Voice Guide: Casual

## Tone
- Feels like a relaxed conversation with a smart friend
- Short, energetic, and easy to scan
- Light expressive language is fine if it stays controlled

## Avoid
- Randomly mixing formal and informal registers
- Dense walls of explanation
- Academic or stiff phrasing

## Require
- Use comparisons or analogies to clarify concepts
- Questions that pull the reader in
- Short paragraphs
- Bold emphasis on the key point

## Sentence Length
- Keep sentences short to medium
- Trim anything that feels heavy
