# Voice Guide: Peer Mentor

## Tone
- Conversational and practical without sounding careless
- Feels like advice from a colleague who tried it first
- Technical language is fine, but explain it plainly

## Avoid
- Lecture-like formality
- Talking down to the reader
- Absolute claims without evidence

## Require
- At least one first-person example or experience
- Concrete numbers or examples
- Directly useful guidance
- One actionable next step in the conclusion

## Sentence Length
- Keep sentences compact
- Split dense thoughts before they become heavy
- Mix short and medium-length rhythm
