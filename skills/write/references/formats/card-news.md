# Card News Format Guide

Use for slide-based narratives where each card advances one step of the story.

## Structure

1. Card 1: headline and hook
2. Card 2-4: evidence, context, or tension
3. Card 5+: implication, takeaway, or action

## Rules

- One idea per card
- Write each card as a complete visual beat
- Maintain narrative momentum from card to card
- Pair claims with numbers, examples, or named entities

## Quality Checks

- Every card has a distinct role in the sequence
- The narrative still makes sense if read card by card
- The final card gives a clear takeaway or action
