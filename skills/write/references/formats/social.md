# Social Format Guide

Use for short, platform-native posts that carry one clear idea.

## Structure

1. Hook in the first line
2. Core insight or claim
3. Proof point, example, or contrast
4. CTA, question, or next action

## Rules

- Keep one post focused on one idea
- Make the opening line skimmable on its own
- Prefer specifics over vague inspiration
- End with a clear reader action when possible

## Quality Checks

- The first line can stand alone as a hook
- The post contains at least one concrete detail
- The CTA matches the platform and audience
